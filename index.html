Our Vision

To see more job creators than job seekers among young Nigerian graduates, drastically reducing unemployment and inspiring a generation that takes the lead.

Our Mission

To equip young Nigerian minds with entrepreneurial skills, innovative thinking, leadership, financial education, and mentorship, preparing graduates to be confident and impactful.

Learn • Grow • Lead

AGWN Methodology:

  • Skills Development: programs, workshops, and Hackathons to enhance graduates' skills.
  • Networking & Mentorship: Pairing graduates with experienced professionals for guidance and support.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Providing resources, financial education, funding, and incubation for aspiring entrepreneurs.
